About

HELPING COIN is the result between a two youngsters discussin about how they can be useful to the society and that too for the poor one's who are not even able to survive due to their financial condition in the society.


HELPING COIN is basically started as an WhatsAPP group in which the people who wish to donate can join and donate RS.50 after every cycle(50 dyas) that is ONE RUPEE A DAY.


HELPING COIN started collecting its first fund from 4 May 2020, where it is created on 14 march 2020.

64% OF WORLD'S EXTREME POOR LIVE IN JUST 5 COUNTRIES: INDIA, CHINA, NIGERIA, BANGLADESH AND CONGO
